Welcome to the unofficial-official website of Rob Curry Drummer (rcroxursox)! Rob, a percussionist since 10, started taking drum set lessons at age 13. Drumming is one of Rob's favorite past times. He posts some of the songs he play on the drums to YouTube to share with others for fun. If you would like to hear a certain song covered by Rob, you can contact him on his YouTube channel or Facebook page!
